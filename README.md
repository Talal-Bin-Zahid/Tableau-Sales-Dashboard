## üìà Sales and Profit Deep Dive - Key Findings

### Product Subcategory Performance

Analysis of the **Sales & Profit by Subcategory** chart reveals strong revenue drivers but also highlights areas of low profitability:

* **Top Sales Subcategories (by Revenue):** The subcategories with the highest total sales volume were **Chairs**, **Phones**, and **Tables**. These are the primary revenue engines for the business.
* **Highest Profit Generators:** Subcategories like **Copiers**, **Machines**, and **Appliances** generated the highest overall profit, indicating strong profit margins or high-volume sales.
* **Low/Negative Profit Areas:** Products like **Furnishings** and **Accessories** show high sales volume but resulted in minimal or negative profit, suggesting potential pricing or cost efficiency issues that require investigation.

### Sales & Profit Trends over Time

The **Sales & Profit Trends over Time** chart tracks performance across the year, with the following observations:

* **Average Profit:** The average profit realized across the measured periods was **$480,833**.
* **Trend Volatility:** Both Sales and Profit lines show significant volatility, with noticeable spikes toward the end of the year, potentially indicating seasonal peaks or successful promotional campaigns.
